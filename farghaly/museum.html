Egypt Museum Tutankhamun

Tutankhamun Interactive Exhibition: An enchanting journey through ancient Egyptian history The Grand Egyptian Museum is proud to host the “Tutankhamun Interactive Exhibition” in partnership with the Spanish Foundation Artes Digitales Madrid. Coinciding with the same month in which the tomb of King Tutankhamun was discovered more than a hundred years ago. The exhibition will present the story of King Tutankhamun in a new and different creative style. Through this interactive experience, and using the latest digital projectors, you will see yourself in the life of the legendary King Tutankhamun, and you will embark on a wonderful audio-visual journey spanning more than 3,400 years of history. As you explore revived temples, treasures and secrets from ancient Egypt you will be surrounded by a 360-degree visual display, coupled with an exceptional soundtrack. As part of your visit, you will also enjoy a guided tour of the Great Staircase, which houses more than 60 unique artifacts of great historical importance, providinga deeper understanding and appreciation of the civilization that gave birth to the legend of King Tutankhamun. Join us in exploring history in a new way and be part of this unforgettable event.
